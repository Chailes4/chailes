My Works

What I’ve Done?

Flippix

Flippix is a simple and effective flashcard reviewer designed to help users study and memorize topics more easily. It supports quick review sessions, customizable flashcards, and efficient learning for any subject.

TastyHub

TastyHub is my capstone project and the one I’m most proud of. It’s a community-focused recipe-sharing platform that helps users discover, create, and share dishes with ease, encouraging creativity and collaboration in cooking.

LikhaPH

Likhaph is an e-commerce platform that highlights handmade Filipino crafts. It connects local artisans with buyers worldwide, promoting creativity and preserving traditional craftsmanship.

CafeCrafts

CafeCrafts is a community-driven website inspired by my love for coffee—especially experimenting with different ingredients and brewing styles. It brings coffee lovers together to share recipes, café creations, and their passion for crafting unique drinks.

CryptoSafe

CryptoSafe is a .NET MAUI cryptocurrency wallet simulator inspired by my personal interest in trading and currency conversion. I built it to mimic real token transfers, helping users (and myself) explore crypto concepts in a safe and interactive way.

TinyEchoes

TinyEchoes is an interactive learning app for kids, designed to make early education fun and engaging. It helps children learn numbers, animals, colors, and basic math through playful visuals, sounds, and simple activities.

PingPong Pro

PingPong Pro is a beginner-friendly learning tool that teaches the basics of table tennis, including rules, techniques, and proper form—perfect for anyone starting their pingpong journey.
